i co-founded and
developed an app to
raise awareness
of the effects of
sea level rise
in miami.

the underwater detector

This featured allows you to take a photo
of your surroundings; then, by using
your phone's geo location, it pulls the
altitude data from Google API, runs an
algorithm, and finds a prediction for
how underwater your current location
will be in 2050 based on
NOAA's estimates.

stories

Once the photos are taken they can be shared on social media and/or in that location's
"story". The main goal is to spread awareness of what the future of
Miami will look like if we do not act now.
